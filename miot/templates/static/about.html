You can create the physical web!

Thy Physical Web is a new and very easy way to interact with physical places and objects. You basically configure beacons to stream URLs of your choice for users to get notified about your content. And they do not even need an app!

This platform called MyInternetOfThings, or miot, is the perfect tool for you to create attractive content about a place or object before configuring your beacons to stream it. And all of this in the same place!

Everything in one single place

Normally, you would have to choose an existing web page in order to stream its URL with your beacon. But the content is not very personal and maybe not perfectly adapted to the concerned physical place or object. Another solution would be to create your own online website, which can be a hard and long task when you're not used to it.

Using this platform, no more troubles! It is the easiest way to create mobile optimized pages of your own to fit perfectly with the concerned place or object. And you can pre-configure your estimote beacon from the same interface! Simply choose the point of interest you just created.

Does it sound good to you? Create an account and immediately become a miot content creator by defining your first points of interest! Physical Web is something new, the more we use it, the greatest it will become. So do not wait any more, join the experience.

Getting started for creators

There are 4 very simple steps to get started with the platform and being able to provide a great experience for users. Do not worry, it is nothing complicated!

1. Get beacons

In order to stream your future URL, you will need beacons. The supported beacons on miot are estimotes. If you have some, you are ready to go. If not, you can get them on the official website. If you do not own or do not want to buy beacons, it is no big deal you can create content anyway without limitations!

Get estimote beacons

2. Create your content

On your dashboard, click Add Point of Interest and fill the informations. You can specify a name, a position, a category, an image and tags. A homepage will automatically get created for it, you can start edit the content and add new pages if needed.

This way, you can actually create a micro website for your physical place or object. Oh! Of course, you can edit the content any time!

Administration dashboard

3. Configure beacon

Create an app from your estimote dashboard and get the credentials. Once you specify these credentials on your miot dashboard, your beacons will be detected and you will be able to preconfigure a few parameters and the streamed URL.

Do not forget to apply modifications

When you update the beacons settings on your dashboard, they are considered as pending settings. You must connect your beacon with the official app to apply the changes!

4. Deploy

Once it is done, simply place the beacon at the concerned place! Users are now ready to get your content.

Discover new content

You can discover the content generated by the users of this platform by browsing the discover page and/or walking near a beacon in physical web mode. These two are described in the following chapters.

1. Browsing the discover page

There are two different layouts available on the discover page. The first one is a basic text page with cases for each point of interest to visit. You can search and filter by category and tags.
